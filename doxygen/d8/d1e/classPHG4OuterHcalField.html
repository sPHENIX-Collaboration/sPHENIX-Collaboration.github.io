Detailed Description

PHG4OuterHcalField.

After burner to produce magnetic field in inner HCal on top of sPHENIX field map In leading order, for the field within the plane of absorber plate, iron absorb almost all the flux, with field in the air-scintillator region reduced to 1/relative_permeability_absorber of that in the iron. The field strength perpendicular to the plate remain unchanged.

relative_permeability_absorber = 1514, relative permeability for Steel 1006 @ B = 1.06T http://www.fieldp.com/magneticproperties.html
